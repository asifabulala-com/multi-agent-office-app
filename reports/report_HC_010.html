Project Plan

Develop a strategic workforce planning system for a telecom company to streamline annual and quarterly headcount planning. The tool will enable department heads to submit headcount requests, validate budgets, map grades/salary bands, track FTE vs contractor splits, and perform scenario modeling, with integrations to Finance and HR systems. Dashboards will serve HR, Finance, and executives; Azure cloud is required.

Total effort: 64hRecommended team: 3 peoplePhases: 2
