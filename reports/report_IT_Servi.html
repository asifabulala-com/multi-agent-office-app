Project Plan

Develop an internal IT Service Management (ITSM) portal for a telecom company's Corporate IT department, enabling incident ticketing, change request workflows, SLA tracking, asset inventory, and a knowledge base. The portal will serve end-users, IT technicians, and IT managers, with strict role-based access and real-time email notifications. Key constraints include integration of dashboards, SLA breach alerts, and robust workflow management.

Total effort: 80hRecommended team: 3 peoplePhases: 2
