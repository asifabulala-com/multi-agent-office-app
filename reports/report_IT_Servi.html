Project Plan

Develop an internal IT Service Management (ITSM) portal for a telecom company's Corporate IT department, enabling incident ticketing, change management, asset inventory, SLA tracking, and a knowledge base. The portal will support end-users, IT technicians, and IT managers, with dashboards and notifications, using Django REST Framework, Vue 3, and PostgreSQL. Key constraints include role-based access, workflow automation, and real-time SLA monitoring.

Total effort: 84hRecommended team: 3 peoplePhases: 2
