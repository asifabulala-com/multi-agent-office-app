Project Plan

Develop an automated payroll processing system for a telecom company, replacing manual Excel workflows with an integrated platform for salary computation, compliance, and direct salary transfers. The system will serve HR and payroll teams, integrate with Oracle HRMS and banking APIs, and ensure UAE Labour Law and MOHRE compliance. Key constraints include regulatory adherence and secure, accurate data handling.

Total effort: 68hRecommended team: 3 peoplePhases: 2
