Project Plan

Develop a centralized HR analytics platform aggregating data from SAP HCM, payroll, recruitment, and learning systems into a unified warehouse, providing real-time, secure workforce insights via Power BI dashboards. The platform will feature predictive attrition modeling and enforce row-level security for business leaders. Key users include CHRO and HR Business Partners, with strict data privacy requirements.

Total effort: 72hRecommended team: 3 peoplePhases: 2
