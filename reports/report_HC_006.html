Project Plan

Develop a digital onboarding system for telecom employees to automate pre-joining and day-one activities, including document collection, IT provisioning, induction scheduling, and compliance acknowledgements. The system targets HR, IT, and new hires, integrating with Active Directory, IT asset management, and payroll, and is built on SharePoint Online with Power Automate and a React front-end.

Total effort: 80hRecommended team: 3 peoplePhases: 2
